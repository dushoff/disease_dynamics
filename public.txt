	The study of the 

		distribution and 

		determinants 

		of health-related states or events 

		in specified populations

	and the application of this study to control health problems
	
	CREDIT J. Last, Dictionary of Epidemiology \url{https://academic.oup.com/aje/article/154/1/93/117432}

Dynamics

	You are at risk if your neighbors have the disease

	Your neighbors are at risk if \emph{their} neighbors have the disease

	Their neighbors are at risk \ldots

	Dynamical models are the tool for understanding this
